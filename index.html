Interactive Mystery Movies

Welcome

Instead of watching your hero solve the crime in a mystery movie, you become the hero and solve the mystery yourself, that is, if you can.

Introduction

Interactive Mystery Movies can be streamed or downloaded from our web server, or you may be able to go to the movie theater and watch one. Interactive Mystery Movies are mystery movies that you solve yourself, or with the help of a fiend, or a group of friends, and since the movies are streamed or downloaded, you can solve the mystery together, no matter where your friends live. We of course have Removed the ending of the movie; you know where we would have our mystery sleuth gather everyone around the drawing room to finally reveal who the guilty person is.

Overview

Interactive Mystery movies are organized into three categories, Beginner, Intermediate, and Advanced, based on how hard they will be to solve, and the type of clues you will be given. Beginner Mystery movies are the easiest to solve, and will give you helpful clues, Intermediate Mystery movies will give you helpful clues, and unhelpful clues, and Advanced Mystery movies will give you helpful clues, unhelpful clues, and misleading clues.
To get started, you will need to go to the Interactive Mystery Movies web site (this site), and log-in to our account server (up soon), and setup your account. Next the account server will ask you to download our Mystery Movies program, or app, you will need this app to receive clues and hints, the app will also keep you up to date on new Mystery Movies coming out.

Clues VS Hints

After you watch one of our Mystery Movie’s, you can use your cell phone or tablet or other device, to log-in to our server, and download your clues. You will receive your clues in the form of a Detective’s package, containing your clues and any pertinent information relating to your assigned case, such as autopsies reports, toxicology reports, fingerprint samples, eyewitness reports, etc.

Clues will usually come in the form of a simple text; however, they could also come as documents or pictures. Documents can come in many different forms, such as marriage and death certificates, wills, or even receipts for items purchased like hunting knifes or chloroform. Pictures or photographs can also be sent to you as a clue, the picture could contain a person of interest, a place of interest, or an item of interest, or something entirely different.

Hints, on the other hand, are for when you get stuck and cannot solve your mystery, and you must request hints, by using our app you can log-in to your account and request a hint to be sent to you. Hints are always helpful, no matter if you are watching a Beginner, Intermediate or Advanced mystery movie. Clues are always helpful only when watching Beginner mystery movies, clues in Intermediate or Advanced mystery movies, may or may not be helpful.

Think You Have Solved Your Mystery

When you think you have solved the mystery, you will need to log into your account using our app, and the server will ask you three (3) questions. You will have to get all three question right in order to solve your mystery. The three questions are -

      1. Who is the guilty person, i.e. – the butler, the cook, the maid, etc.

      2. Where was the crime committed, i.e. - the kitchen, the bedroom, etc.

      3. What method was used, i.e. - a gun, poison, etc.
